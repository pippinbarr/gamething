GAMETHING is a series of answerphone messages between Pippin Barr and David Wolinsky that explores videogames from top to bottom. Each season focuses on a theme, such as simulation or horror and discusses one game per episode to unpack that theme. From jiggling drumsticks to the inability to sorry as a police officer, GAMETHING has it all.

Pippin Barr

Pippin Barr is an Associate Professor of Computation Arts at Concordia University in Montréal. He makes experimental games about anything from performance art to chess variations to classic films.

David Wolinsky

David Wolinsky is an author, recovering critic, and creator/curator of the interview series Don't Die, preserved by Stanford. He's also a former Onion and NBC editor. If you see him, say "hi."
