GAMETHING is answerphone messages between Pippin Barr and David Wolinsky that explore videogames from top to bottom. Each season explores a specific theme, such as simulation or horror, and discusses one game per episode. The physics of loneliness? Police officers who can't say sorry? Death by dysentery? GAMETHING has it all.

In Season 3, GAMETHING takes on simulators and simulations in the world of games. From handling traffic accidents as a police officer to romancing Colonel Sanders to schlepping down the Oregon Trail, there's a gamething for everyone.

David Wolinsky is an author, recovering critic, and creator/curator of Don't Die (2014-present), a Stanford-preserved and award-winning interview series exploring alternate histories of the internet, videogames, and the re-negotiation of power made possible and inevitable by our new systems for socializing, working, and interacting. He's also a former Onion and NBC editor. If you see him, say "hi."

David is the 1995 Blockbuster store champion at Donkey Kong Country and author of the forthcoming oral history All The Rage: Conversations Processing Gamergate.
